About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Meida and Communication, City University of Hong Kong. I received my PhD in information studies from Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, Nanyang Technological University.

My research converges at the intersection of communication, information, and technology, echoing my education background, an intersection of arts and technology. My research interests focus on Consumer Health Informatics, Social Networks, Human-Computer Interaction, and Social Media.
